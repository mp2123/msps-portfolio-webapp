# Site Surface Strategy

This repo now supports three distinct portfolio/media surfaces with different jobs.

## 1. `portfolio-app`

This is the primary public portfolio and current career surface.

- Audience: recruiters, hiring managers, collaborators, and professional referrals
- Current leaning: analytics, BI, automation, and operator-to-analyst positioning
- Primary goals: communicate role fit, show proof, surface projects, and convert to conversation

## 2. Future bartender recruiter portfolio

This is a planned later surface and should stay separate from `portfolio-app`.

- Audience: bartending and hospitality recruiters / hiring managers
- Focus: service craft, hospitality leadership, bar operations, speed, guest experience, training, and beverage knowledge
- Goal: let the bartending story stand on its own without competing with the analytics narrative

## 3. `bartender-app`

This is the customer/following/affiliate-media site, not the recruiter portfolio.

- Audience: cocktail enthusiasts, social followers, affiliate traffic, and video/content viewers
- Focus: recipes, bar tools, content, affiliate links, social funnels, signup flows, and media growth
- Goal: build audience and monetizable attention rather than hiring-market positioning

## Working rule

- `portfolio-app` stays career-first.
- The future bartender recruiter portfolio stays recruiting-first for hospitality.
- `bartender-app` stays audience/content/affiliate-first.

Components can inspire each other, but the product purpose of each surface should remain distinct.
